Welcome to a CS 279 Experiment

Thank you for volunteering to participate in this experiment. This experiment will be in three parts. In the first part, you will be asked several demographic questions.

In the second part, you will practice a task. Finally, you will be asked to complete two tasks that will be similar to the practice task. You will have the opportunity to take breaks in between the tasks.

This experiment is based on a paper called Ephemeral Adaptation: The Use of Gradual Onset to Improve Menu Selection Performance.
